Seth Charleston

Seth Charleston makes eclectic indie rock stemming from a wide variety of influences. His songs are crafted with a strong focus on groove, melody, and intricate instrumentation. As a 17-year-old, he released his debut project, titled the Waiting for the Day EP. Released independently on March 16th, 2018, the EP showcased a raw but promising indie rock sound.

The rest of 2018 saw the releases of singles Carpe Noctem and Jungle, respectively. Both songs marked additional steps in the right direction towards his artistic development. Jungle in particular marked a turning point for Seth. Not only did it bring forth a different sound, it more importantly was released in conjunction with his move from his Wisconsin hometown to Los Angeles, CA.

Now based in LA, Seth continues to evolve his sound. Lightseeker, his first release of 2019, demonstrates that growth and maturation firsthand.
